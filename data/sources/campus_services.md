# Campus Services

## Library Hours
The main library is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and on
weekends (Saturday and Sunday) from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. During exam weeks the
library stays open until midnight. The library is closed on public holidays.

## Borrowing Books
Students can borrow up to 6 books at a time for 14 days, renewable twice online
through the library catalog, provided no one else has reserved the book.

## Computer Labs
The department computer labs are open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays.
Your student ID card is required for entry.

## Wi-Fi Access
Connect to the "Campus-WiFi" network and log in with your student portal
credentials. For connection problems, contact the IT helpdesk at
ithelp@college.edu.
