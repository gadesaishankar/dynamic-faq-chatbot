# Fees & Administration

## Paying Tuition Fees
Tuition fees can be paid online through the student portal under "Fee Payment"
using net banking, credit/debit card, or UPI. You can also pay by demand draft
at the accounts office. Fees for each semester are due before the registration
window closes. A late fee applies after the due date.

## Fee Receipts
After an online payment, a receipt is generated instantly and emailed to your
registered address. You can re-download receipts anytime from the portal.

## Scholarships
Merit and need-based scholarships are available. Applications open at the start
of each academic year; check the "Scholarships" section of the portal for
eligibility and deadlines.

## ID Cards
Lost ID cards can be replaced at the administration office for a small fee.
Bring a passport-size photo and your enrollment number.

## Transcripts and Certificates
Official transcripts and bonafide certificates can be requested through the
portal under "Documents". Processing takes 3-5 working days.
