# Academics & Registration

## Course Registration
Students register for courses through the student portal at portal.college.edu
during the registration window announced each semester. Log in, open "Course
Registration", select your subjects for the semester, and submit. Registration
for the upcoming semester typically opens two weeks before classes begin. If a
course is full, you can join its waitlist from the same screen.

## Adding or Dropping Courses
You may add or drop courses during the first week of the semester (the
add/drop period) without penalty through the student portal. After the add/drop
period, dropping a course requires approval from your academic advisor.

## Academic Advising
Every student is assigned a faculty advisor. Book an advising appointment
through the portal under "Advising". Advisors help with course selection,
degree planning, and academic difficulties.

## Exam Schedule
The end-semester exam timetable is published on the department notice board and
the portal about three weeks before exams begin.
